About Us

Welcome to Hybrid Drone Services! My passion for technology and aerial imaging led me to create a business that makes high-quality drone services accessible and easy to book. I specialize in providing aerial solutions for agriculture, construction, and sports filming, helping farmers monitor crop health, construction managers track site progress, and coaches capture game footage from unique perspectives. My goal is to deliver reliable, professional results while giving customers a simple way to schedule services, view updates, and explore examples of past work. About Me

Hi, I'm Jack Snidanko, the founder of Hybrid Drone Services and a Part 107 licensed pilot. My journey with drones began as a hobby, experimenting with drones as a kid. Over time, that passion grew as I used drones more and more. I did robotics in high school which pushed me to love drones. Whether it's capturing stunning landscape footage, assisting farmers with crop monitoring, or supporting local sports teams, I take pride in combining creativity with precision to deliver exceptional results.
